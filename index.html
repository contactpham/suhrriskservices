COO & Director of Risk Services

Peter Leary, MBA, CRIS, CISR

As President of Suhr Risk Services, Peter is responsible for driving the firm's organic growth while maintaining the integrity and culture built by the Suhr family. Additionally, Peter drives the corporate operations and all mergers and acquisition activity in Northern California.

Prior to joining Suhr Risk Services, Peter served as Vice President of Marsh McLennan’s Phoenix and Hartford regional offices for over 8 years. While at Marsh, Peter was responsible for handling the risk management program for the City of Las Vegas and the Las Vegas Metro Police Department. His career also includes over 8 years serving as Underwriting Manager for Liberty Mutual’ s middle and small market initiatives in Massachusetts, New Hampshire, Indiana and Arizona. During his tenure at Liberty Mutual, Peter was instrumental in the acquisition of Golden Eagles commercial portfolio as well as handling the workers' compensation program for Knight Transportation and Ping.

Mr. Leary holds a bachelor’s degree in economics from the University of Massachusetts, Dartmouth and a master’s degree in finance from the Anna Maria College of Management in Paxton, Massachusetts where he served as tennis coach and Dean/Director of Student Life.

Peter has three children, Brenden, Keegan, and Reganne and enjoys time outside of the office with his better half Theresa.
