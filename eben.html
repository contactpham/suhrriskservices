Gerry Kosko, CHRS, HIP

As Senior Account Executive of the Benefits Division at Suhr Risk Services, Gerry Kosko plays an instrumental role in the management and execution of our employee benefits approach. With over 10 years of experience in the field, Gerry has become an expert in Employee Benefits, Partial Self-Funded programs, and Health Reimbursement Arrangements.

A former partner at Petrick & Kosko Insurance Services, a subsidiary of Archway Insurance, Gerry was responsible for starting their benefits division. In 2014, Gerry joined Suhr Risk Services to further expand our growing regional and national employee benefits portfolio. His experience in managing a diverse portfolio of clients, including the San Jose Sharks, has given him the expertise and insight that allows him to implement strategies which have improved benefits and saved money for clients and their employees. He has a number of designations: Certified Healthcare Reform Specialist (CHRS), Health Insurance Professional (HIP), and is currently working on his Group Benefits Advisor (GBA) designation.

Gerry is a current member, and former board member, of the Rotary Club of Santa Clara. He serves as the Event Chair for the Silicon Valley BBQ Championships and the Santa Clara Octoberfest. When not active in his community, Gerry enjoys playing bocce ball and is also an avid golfer and angler.
