Peter Leary, MBA, CRIS, CISR

As President of Suhr Risk Services, Peter is responsible for driving the firm's organic growth while maintaining the integrity and culture built by the Suhr family. Additionally, Peter drives the corporate operations and all mergers and acquisition activity in Northern California.

Prior to joining Suhr Risk Services, Peter served as Vice President of Marsh McLennan’s Phoenix and Hartford regional offices for over 8 years. While at Marsh, Peter was responsible for handling the risk management program for the City of Las Vegas and the Las Vegas Metro Police Department. His career also includes over 8 years serving as Underwriting Manager for Liberty Mutual’ s middle and small market initiatives in Massachusetts, New Hampshire, Indiana and Arizona. During his tenure at Liberty Mutual, Peter was instrumental in the acquisition of Golden Eagles commercial portfolio as well as handling the workers' compensation program for Knight Transportation and Ping.

Mr. Leary holds a bachelor’s degree in economics from the University of Massachusetts, Dartmouth and a master’s degree in finance from the Anna Maria College of Management in Paxton, Massachusetts where he served as tennis coach and Dean/Director of Student Life.

Peter has three children, Brenden, Keegan, and Reganne and enjoys time outside of the office with his better half Theresa.

Sheldon A. Beitzel, CIC, CLCS, CWCA, CRIS


Senior Vice President

As Senior Vice President, Sheldon Beitzel focuses on Construction and Wholesale Distribution, and is an overall Workers’ Compensation specialist. Considered as a results-driven partner, Sheldon provides expert risk management and insurance solutions that his clients trust. His sought-after understanding of property and casualty insurance has led to a number of invitations: He has been a featured speaker for Skanska during the BART Berryessa Extension - Surety Bonding with Minority Owned Businesses assembly, a featured speaker for the Turner School of Construction, and a featured speaker for Surety & Emerging Businesses assembly for the California Water Association.

Sheldon is committed to the industries and communities he serves, volunteering his time to three different boards, all of which focus on low-income housing and minority business advocacy: ASIAN, Inc., Asian American Contractors Association, and Build Bayview. In addition, he is a member of the Guardsmen, a non-profit organization which provides low or no cost funding for at-risk youth to attend summer camp as well as scholarships.

A true insurance expert, Sheldon has attained well-regarded professional designations: Certified Insurance Counselor (CIC), Construction Risk Insurance Specialist (CRIS), Commercial Lines Coverage Specialist (CLCS), and Certified Workers’ Compensation Advisor (CWCA). He also holds a bachelor’s degree in Business Administration & Marketing from Sonoma State University.
